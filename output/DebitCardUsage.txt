!!! __DebitCardUsage__

__Debit Card Usage__ refers to the practice of using a debit card to make purchases by deducting funds directly from the user's checking account linked to the card.

When it comes to managing investment accounts, understanding how and when to use a debit card is important. Improper usage can lead to overspending, which might affect your ability to invest effectively or meet financial goals.
