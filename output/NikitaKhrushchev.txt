!!! Nikita Khrushchev

__Nikita Khrushchev__ (1894-1971) was a Soviet politician who served as the leader of the Soviet Union from 1953 to 1964. He is best known for his role in initiating de-Stalinization and easing tensions with Western powers, although he also faced significant challenges such as the [[BerlinCrisisOf1961]].
