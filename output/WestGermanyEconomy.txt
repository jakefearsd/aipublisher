!!! __WestGermanyEconomy__

The __WestGermanyEconomy__ refers to the economic system of West Germany (the Federal Republic of Germany) from its establishment in 1949 until German reunification in 1990. It was characterized by a strong emphasis on free market principles, social welfare policies, and export-oriented industrialization. This economy played a crucial role in rebuilding post-war Europe and had significant implications for the broader European economic landscape during the Cold War era.
