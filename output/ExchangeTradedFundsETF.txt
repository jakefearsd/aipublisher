!!! ExchangeTradedFunds (ETF)

An __Exchange Traded Fund__ (ETF) is a type of financial security that tracks an index, commodity, bond, or a basket of assets like an index fund but trades on an exchange like an individual stock. ETFs offer investors a way to diversify their portfolios and gain exposure to various markets with lower fees compared to traditional mutual funds.
