!!! __ModernismAndProgressivism__

__Modernism And Progressivism__ are related but distinct intellectual and artistic movements of the early to mid-20th century, particularly influential in Europe. Modernism emphasized a break from traditional forms and values in literature, art, architecture, and music, while Progressivism focused on social reform and improving living conditions through political and economic changes.
