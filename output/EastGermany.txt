!!! EastGermany

__East Germany__, officially known as the German Democratic Republic (GDR), was a state established in 1949 in the Soviet occupation zone of Germany after World War II. It existed until its reunification with West Germany on October 3, 1990, following the fall of the Berlin Wall in 1989. East Germany was characterized by a single-party communist government and close ties to the Soviet Union.
