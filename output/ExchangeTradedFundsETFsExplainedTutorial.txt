!!! __Exchange-Traded Funds (ETFs) Explained Tutorial__

An exchange-traded fund (ETF) is a type of investment fund that holds assets such as stocks, bonds, commodities, or other financial instruments and trades on an exchange like a common stock. ETFs offer investors the diversification and low costs of mutual funds with the flexibility of trading individual stocks.
