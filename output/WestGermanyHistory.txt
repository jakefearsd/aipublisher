!!! __WestGermanyHistory__

__West Germany History__ refers to the political, social, and economic development of West Germany (officially known as the Federal Republic of Germany) from its founding in 1949 after World War II until German reunification in 1990. This period saw significant reconstruction efforts, economic growth through the Marshall Plan and the creation of the European Economic Community, political stability within a divided Europe, and cultural developments that shaped modern Germany.
