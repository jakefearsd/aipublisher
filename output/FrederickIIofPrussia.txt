!!! Frederick II of Prussia

__Frederick II of Prussia__, also known as Frederick the Great (1712-1786), was a King of Prussia from 1740 until his death in 1786. He is renowned for his military prowess, enlightened reforms, and cultural patronage. Under his rule, Berlin became a significant cultural center, attracting artists, philosophers, and scholars.
