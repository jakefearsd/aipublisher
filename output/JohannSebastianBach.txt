!!! __Johann Sebastian Bach__

__Johann Sebastian Bach__ (1685-1750) was a German composer and musician of the Baroque period. His works include the Brandenburg Concertos, Mass in B minor, The Well-Tempered Clavier, and many cantatas, oratorios, and organ pieces.

Although primarily known for his contributions to classical music, Bach's legacy is referenced in discussions on historical branding and artistic influence, such as the context of the [Brandenburg Concertos] in the article [Historical Background Of Brandenburg].
