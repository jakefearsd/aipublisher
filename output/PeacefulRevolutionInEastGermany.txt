!!! __Peaceful Revolution in East Germany__

The __Peaceful Revolution__ (in German: _BÃ¼rgerbewegung_ or _friedliche Revolution_) was a series of pro-democracy demonstrations and civil resistance movements that took place primarily in East Germany, with Berlin as its epicenter, from September 1989 to March 1990. The revolution led to the fall of the Berlin Wall on November 9, 1989, and subsequently to German reunification.
