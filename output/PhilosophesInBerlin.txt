!!! PhilosophesInBerlin

__PhilosophesInBerlin__ refers to the intellectual circle of philosophers and thinkers who gathered in Berlin during the reign of Frederick II (Frederick the Great) in the 18th century. This period was marked by an influx of Enlightenment thinkers seeking a vibrant cultural environment that fostered debate, innovation, and philosophical discourse.
