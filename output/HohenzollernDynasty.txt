!!! __Hohenzollern Dynasty__

The __Hohenzollern Dynasty__ was a noble German family that ruled Brandenburg and later Prussia, becoming one of the most influential royal houses in Europe from the 15th century through World War I. The dynasty played a significant role in shaping modern Germany's history, including its political and military developments.
