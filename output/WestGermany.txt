!!! WestGermany

__West Germany__, officially known as the Federal Republic of Germany (FRG), was a country established in 1949 after World War II in the western zones occupied by Allied forces. It consisted of the three Western occupation zones and was bordered by East Germany, which led to significant political and cultural contrasts between the two German states.
