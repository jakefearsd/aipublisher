!!! Congress for Cultural Freedom

__Congress for Cultural Freedom__ was an international committee established in 1950 during the early years of the Cold War to promote Western culture and ideas against Soviet influence. It played a significant role in cultural diplomacy, supporting artists, intellectuals, and publications across Europe.
