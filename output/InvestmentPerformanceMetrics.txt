!!! InvestmentPerformanceMetrics

__Investment Performance Metrics__ are quantitative measures used to evaluate and report on the financial success of an investment over a specified period. These metrics help investors understand how well their investments have performed compared to benchmarks or other similar investments.

* Examples include return on investment, Sharpe ratio, alpha, and beta.
* These metrics provide insights into risk-adjusted returns, volatility, and market sensitivity.
