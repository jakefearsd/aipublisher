!!! Marshall Plan

The __Marshall Plan__ was an American initiative passed in 1948 for rebuilding and creating a stronger financial foundation for the countries of Europe, which lost significant resources during World War II. It provided over $12 billion ($130 billion adjusted for inflation in 2020) to help rebuild European economies and prevent the spread of Soviet communism.
