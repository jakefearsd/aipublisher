!!! __ColdWarCulture__

__Cold War Culture__ refers to the distinct cultural environment that emerged in Berlin during the Cold War era (1945-1963), heavily influenced by the city's division into East and West. This culture was characterized by a blend of ideological confrontation, artistic expression, and daily life under the shadow of political tension.
