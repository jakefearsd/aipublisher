!!! __UrbanPlanningandArchitectureunderFrederickWilliamITutorial__

__Urban Planning and Architecture under Frederick William I Tutorial__ is an educational guide focusing on the urban development principles and architectural styles introduced during the reign of Frederick William I (1688-1740), King of Prussia. This tutorial explores how his policies shaped the cityscapes of Brandenburg and Prussia, emphasizing military fortifications, baroque aesthetics, and practical urban planning.
