!!! MarketTradingPlatforms

A __Market Trading Platform__ is an online service that allows users to buy and sell financial instruments such as stocks, bonds, commodities, currencies, and other securities. These platforms provide traders with real-time market data, trading tools, and often offer various types of orders and investment products like ETFs (Exchange Traded Funds).
