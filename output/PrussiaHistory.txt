!!! __PrussiaHistory__

__Prussia History__ refers to the historical development of Prussia, a state that played a significant role in German history from the 17th century until its dissolution after World War II. The history of Prussia is marked by periods of military expansion and political influence, particularly under leaders like Frederick the Great.
