!!! __Defined Contribution Plans__

A __defined contribution plan__ is an employer-sponsored retirement savings program where contributions are made by both the employee and sometimes the employer into a personal account for each participant. The final amount available at retirement depends on factors such as the contributions made, investment performance, and time horizon.
