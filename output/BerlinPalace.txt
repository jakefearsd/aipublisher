!!! Berlin Palace

__Berlin Palace__ was a significant royal residence in [[Berlin]], serving as the principal residence of the electors and kings of Prussia from its construction until it was destroyed by fire at the end of World War II. Its architecture played an important role in shaping the city's skyline and cultural identity during the reign of Frederick II, also known as Frederick the Great.
