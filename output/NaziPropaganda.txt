!!! Nazi Propaganda

__Nazi Propaganda__ refers to the extensive and systematic dissemination of information and cultural products by the National Socialist German Workers' Party (NSDAP) in Germany during the 1930s and early 1940s. This propaganda aimed to shape public opinion, promote Nazi ideology, and suppress dissenting views. In Berlin, the capital city under Nazi rule, propaganda was a crucial tool for maintaining control over the population and mobilizing support for the regime's policies.
