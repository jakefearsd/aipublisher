!!! __European History 18th Century__

__European History 18th Century__ refers to the historical period from 1701 to 1800 during which significant political, social, and cultural changes occurred across Europe. This era witnessed the Enlightenment, the American Revolution, and the rise of absolutist monarchies alongside burgeoning revolutionary ideas.
