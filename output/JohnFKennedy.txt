!!! __JohnFKennedy__

__John F. Kennedy__ was the 35th President of the United States from 1961 to 1963. He is known for his leadership during significant Cold War events, including the [Berlin Crisis of 1961], which tested his diplomatic and strategic capabilities early in his presidency.
