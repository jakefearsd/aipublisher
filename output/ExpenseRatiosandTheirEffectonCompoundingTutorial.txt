!!! Expense Ratios and Their Effect on Compounding Tutorial

__Expense ratios__ refer to the annual fee charged by a mutual fund or an exchange-traded fund (ETF) to cover its operating expenses and management fees, expressed as a percentage of assets. Understanding how these ratios impact compounding is crucial for investors aiming to maximize their returns over time.

* Expense ratios can significantly erode investment gains through compounding interest, especially over long periods.
* This tutorial explains the mechanics behind expense ratios and provides insights on how minimizing them can enhance overall portfolio performance.
