!!! __LGBTQ Culture in Berlin__

__LGBTQ Culture in Berlin__ refers to the vibrant and diverse community of lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, and other sexual and gender minorities that have flourished in Berlin since the early 20th century. Known for its liberal attitudes and progressive policies, Berlin has become a global hub for LGBTQ rights and culture.
