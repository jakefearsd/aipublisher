!!! Prussian Army Reform

__Prussian Army Reform__ refers to a series of military reforms implemented in the Kingdom of Prussia during the mid-18th century, primarily under King Frederick II (Frederick the Great). These reforms were instrumental in transforming the Prussian army into one of the most effective and disciplined forces in Europe. The reforms focused on improving training, logistics, and command structure, significantly enhancing Prussia's military capabilities.
