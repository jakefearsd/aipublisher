!!! CreditCardFees

__Credit Card Fees__ refer to charges levied by credit card issuers for various services and actions taken by cardholders beyond just regular transactions. These fees can include late payment penalties, balance transfer costs, annual membership dues, cash advance fees, foreign transaction charges, and more.

Understanding these fees is crucial as they can significantly impact the total cost of using a credit card and affect one's financial planning and investment strategies.
