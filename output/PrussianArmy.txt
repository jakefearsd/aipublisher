!!! Prussian Army

The __Prussian Army__ was the military force of the Kingdom of Prussia from 1701 until its dissolution in 1918 after World War I. It played a crucial role in Prussia's rise as one of the major powers in Europe and had significant influence on German military thought and tactics.
