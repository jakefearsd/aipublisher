!!! GreenSpacesBerlin

__GreenSpacesBerlin__ refers to the network of parks, gardens, and other public green areas in Berlin that serve as vital components for urban planning and community well-being. These spaces are crucial for enhancing environmental sustainability, promoting physical activity, and fostering social cohesion within one of Europe's largest cities.
