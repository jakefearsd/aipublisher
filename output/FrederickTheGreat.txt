!!! FrederickTheGreat

__FrederickTheGreat__, also known as Frederick II of Prussia (1712-1786), was a King of Prussia from 1740 until his death in 1786. He was an influential military and political leader, who significantly expanded the territory and influence of Prussia through strategic alliances and successful military campaigns, most notably during the Seven Years' War.
