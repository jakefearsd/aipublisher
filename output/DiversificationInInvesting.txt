!!! Diversification In Investing

*Diversification in investing* is a strategy that involves allocating funds among different types of investments to spread risk. This approach helps mitigate the impact of poor performance in any single investment and aims to enhance returns through better risk management.

The concept of diversification is fundamental in building balanced portfolios, which can include stocks, bonds, real estate, and other asset classes. By investing across various sectors or geographic regions, investors seek to reduce the volatility of their overall portfolio.
