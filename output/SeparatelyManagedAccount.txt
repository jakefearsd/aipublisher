!!! Separately Managed Account

A **Separately Managed Account (SMA)** is an investment account where a professional money manager has full discretion over which securities are bought and sold within the account on behalf of one individual investor, usually an institutional client such as a pension fund or high net worth individual. Unlike mutual funds or other pooled investments, SMAs offer personalized portfolios with direct ownership of underlying assets.

* Tailored to meet specific investment goals.
* Provides transparency as investors own the securities directly.
* Offers tax benefits and customization options for clients.
