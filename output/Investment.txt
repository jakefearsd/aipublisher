!!! Investment

__Investment__ refers to the act of allocating resources, usually money, with the expectation of generating income or profit within a specified period. This can involve purchasing assets such as stocks, bonds, real estate, or starting a business venture.

* * *

Investments are an essential part of financial planning and wealth creation for individuals and organizations alike.
