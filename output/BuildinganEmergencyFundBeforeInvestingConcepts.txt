!!! BuildinganEmergencyFundBeforeInvestingConcepts

__Building an Emergency Fund Before Investing Concepts__ refers to the financial advice and strategies that prioritize saving a reserve of cash before engaging in investment activities. This approach emphasizes the importance of having a financial safety net to cover unexpected expenses, thereby reducing the likelihood of needing to liquidate investments at potentially unfavorable times.

* Context: Establishing this fund is often recommended as a foundational step towards building a secure financial future and achieving long-term investing goals.
