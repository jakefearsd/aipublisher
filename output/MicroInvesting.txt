!!! MicroInvesting

__MicroInvesting__ is an investing strategy that involves making small, frequent investments in financial assets like stocks, bonds, and mutual funds. This approach allows individuals with limited capital to start building a diversified portfolio over time. Micro-investing platforms often offer automated services to make the process convenient for users.
