!!! Prussia Under Frederick William I

__Prussia Under Frederick William I__ refers to the period in which the Kingdom of Prussia was ruled by King Frederick William I from 1713 to 1740. Known for his militaristic policies and austere rule, Frederick William I focused on expanding the Prussian army and bureaucracy while emphasizing strict discipline and frugality.
