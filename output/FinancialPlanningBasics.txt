!!! Financial Planning Basics

__Financial planning basics__ refer to the fundamental principles and strategies involved in managing personal finances effectively. This includes setting financial goals, budgeting, saving, investing, and planning for retirement and other life events.

* It forms the foundation for understanding how to make informed decisions about money management and investment.
* Effective financial planning helps individuals achieve financial stability and security over time.
