!!! Economic Impact of the Cold War on Berlin Tutorial

__Economic Impact of the Cold War on Berlin Tutorial__ is a guide that explores how the geopolitical tensions between the Eastern and Western blocs during the Cold War period affected the economic development, infrastructure, and daily life in divided Berlin. This tutorial aims to provide an understanding of the specific challenges and opportunities that emerged for East and West Berliners as a result of the ideological divide.
