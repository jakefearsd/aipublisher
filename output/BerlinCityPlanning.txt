!!! BerlinCityPlanning

__Berlin City Planning__ refers to the systematic approach and strategies employed for the development, growth, and renovation of the city of Berlin, Germany. This planning encompasses a wide range of urban development activities including housing, transportation, public spaces, and environmental sustainability.
