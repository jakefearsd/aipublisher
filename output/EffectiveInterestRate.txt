!!! EffectiveInterestRate

__Effective Interest Rate__ is the rate of interest that accounts for the effect of compounding over a given period. It represents the true cost of borrowing or the actual return on investment when interest compounds more than once per year.

The effective interest rate is crucial in financial calculations as it provides a more accurate measure of the time value of money, which is essential for making informed decisions in investments and loans.
