!!! __401kPlan__

A 401(k) plan is an employer-sponsored retirement savings account that allows employees in the United States to save and invest a portion of their income on a pre-tax basis, thereby reducing current taxable income. Employers often match contributions up to a certain percentage, providing additional incentive for participation.
