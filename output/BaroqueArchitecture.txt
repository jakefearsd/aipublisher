!!! __Baroque Architecture__

__Baroque Architecture__ is an elaborate and dynamic style of architecture that emerged in Italy during the early 17th century and spread throughout Europe, including Berlin, by the mid-17th century. Characterized by its grandeur, symmetry, and dramatic use of light and shadow, Baroque architecture sought to evoke awe and emotional response from viewers through ornate decoration and complex forms.
