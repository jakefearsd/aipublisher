!!! Cold War Timeline

__Cold War Timeline__ refers to the chronological sequence of significant events that marked the geopolitical and ideological rivalry between the Western democracies led by the United States and the Eastern communist bloc led by the Soviet Union, which lasted from the end of World War II in 1945 until the dissolution of the Soviet Union in 1991. This timeline includes major political, economic, and military events that shaped international relations during this period.
