!!! __EastGermanyHistory__

__East Germany History__ refers to the period from 1949 to 1990 when East Germany, officially known as the German Democratic Republic (GDR), existed as a state separate from West Germany. This era was marked by significant political, economic, and social changes influenced by its socialist government and relations with the Soviet Union.
