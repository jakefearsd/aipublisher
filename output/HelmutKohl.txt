!!! Helmut Kohl

__Helmut Kohl__ was a German statesman and member of the Christian Democratic Union (CDU) who served as Chancellor of West Germany from 1982 to 1990, and subsequently as Chancellor of reunified Germany until 1998. He played a pivotal role in the fall of the Berlin Wall and the subsequent reunification of East and West Germany.
