!!! Inflation Rate Calculation

__Inflation Rate Calculation__ refers to the process of determining how much prices have increased over a specific period in an economy. This calculation helps measure the decline in purchasing power and is crucial for investors, economists, and policymakers.

* Formula: The most common formula used is (CPI current period - CPI previous period) / CPI previous period * 100, where CPI stands for Consumer Price Index.
  
* Purpose: It aids in understanding real returns on investments by accounting for the decrease in value of money over time due to inflation.
