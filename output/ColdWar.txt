!!! Cold War

The __Cold War__ was a period of geopolitical tension between the [[Superpower|Rival superpowers]] following World War II, primarily between the United States and the Soviet Union. This conflict lasted from the late 1940s until the early 1990s and involved political, economic, and military conflicts rather than direct warfare.
