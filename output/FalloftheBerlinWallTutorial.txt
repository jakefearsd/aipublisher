!!! Fall of the Berlin Wall Tutorial

__Fall of the Berlin Wall Tutorial__ refers to educational resources designed to provide an overview and deeper understanding of the historical event when the physical wall dividing East and West Berlin was opened on November 9, 1989.

The tutorial covers key events leading up to the fall, its immediate consequences, and long-term impacts on Germany and Europe. It aims to help readers grasp the significance of this moment in Cold War history and its implications for international relations and politics.
