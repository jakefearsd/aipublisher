!!! Berlin Cathedral

__Berlin Cathedral__ (officially known as ''Erbprinzpalais'' and formerly called the __Domkirche Friedrichstadt__) is a historical monument in [[Berlin]], Germany. It is not actually a cathedral but rather a Lutheran church located on Museum Island, near the Lustgarten park. The building has been an important landmark since its construction in the 18th century and continues to serve as a significant cultural and religious site today.
