!!! EnlightenmentPhilosophy

__Enlightenment Philosophy__ refers to the intellectual movement that emphasized reason, individualism, and skepticism during the 17th and 18th centuries in Europe. This philosophical approach challenged traditional authority and superstition, promoting science, democracy, and human rights. In Berlin, under the reign of Frederick II (Frederick the Great), Enlightenment ideas flourished, influencing the cultural and intellectual landscape of Prussian society.
