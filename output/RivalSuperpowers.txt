!!! Rival Superpowers

__Rival superpowers__ refer to major world powers that are in conflict or competition with each other during a specific historical period. This term is often used to describe nations like the United States and the Soviet Union during the Cold War era, where both were engaged in a global rivalry for dominance.

* * *

In the context of Berlin history from 1500 to 2020, the concept of rival superpowers becomes particularly relevant during the Cold War period, when the division of Berlin into East and West became a focal point of the broader geopolitical struggle between the United States and the Soviet Union.
