!!! __Enlightenment Culture in Europe__

__Enlightenment Culture in Europe__ refers to the intellectual and cultural movement that flourished across Europe during the 17th and 18th centuries, emphasizing reason, individualism, and scientific inquiry over traditional religious dogma. This period was marked by significant advancements in philosophy, science, literature, and politics, influencing social structures and thought processes throughout the continent.
