!!! __MargraviateOfBrandenburg__

The __Margraviate of Brandenburg__ was a [[historical state in Central Europe|state]] that existed from 1134 to 1618, serving as an important entity within the Holy Roman Empire. Initially a march, or border province, it later became a margraviate under the Ascanian dynasty and played a significant role in German history.
