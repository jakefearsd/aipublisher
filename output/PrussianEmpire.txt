!!! __Prussian Empire__

The term ''__Prussian Empire__'' is not accurate as Prussia never officially declared itself an empire; however, it was a powerful state within the German Empire from 1871 to 1918. Before this period, Prussia was a leading state in modern Germany and played a crucial role in shaping Berlin's political and cultural landscape.
