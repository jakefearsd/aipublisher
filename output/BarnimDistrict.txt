!!! Barnim District

__Barnim District__ is an administrative district located in Brandenburg, Germany. It covers a significant area of land north of Berlin and includes parts of historical Prussian regions. The district has a rich history intertwined with its geographical location, playing a role in the development of Brandenburg.
