!!! __MilitaryArchitectureInEurope__

__Military Architecture in Europe__ refers to the design and construction of military buildings and fortifications across European countries from ancient times through the modern era. This includes various structures such as castles, forts, citadels, defensive walls, and strategic urban layouts designed to protect against invasions or sieges.
